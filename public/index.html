About My First Golf Club

🏆

The Mission

Every golfer has an origin story — a first swing, a first course, a person who put a club in their hand. My First Golf Club is a living registry that preserves these moments for individuals and organizations alike. We believe these stories connect us across generations, borders, and handicaps.

🃏

The Cards

Inspired by baseball collector cards, each card captures the essential stats and story of a golf origin. Player Cards are for individuals. Club Cards are for organizations, golf clubs, academies, and corporates. Cards earn era badges based on when the journey started — Vintage, Classic, or Modern.

🌎

The Community

Our Community Wall brings together origin stories from around the world. Browse, search, and discover how golf found its way into the lives of players and institutions everywhere. Every card added makes the tapestry richer.

🚀

What's Next

Your clubhouse is now live — log rounds, earn XP, unlock badges, and build your Top 100 collection. Coming soon: shareable card images for social media, a global timeline view, regional collections, endorsements between members, and verified badges for established clubs. The registry grows with you.
